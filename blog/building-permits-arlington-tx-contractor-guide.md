## Arlington's Building Permit System

Arlington processes permits through the **City of Arlington Development Services Department**, an active office managing thousands of permits annually for residential, commercial, and industrial projects throughout the city and surrounding growth areas.

### Where to Apply

**Online (Recommended):** Arlington offers online permit submission through their portal at **arlingtontx.gov/development**. This is the fastest and most convenient method. The system provides real-time status tracking and automated updates throughout the review process. Most contractors prefer online submission for efficiency and documentation.

**In-Person:** Visit the Development Services office at **101 West Main Street, Arlington, TX 76010** (Downtown). Hours are Monday-Friday, 7 AM to 5 PM. Peak hours (8-10 AM) can mean 20-40 minute wait times. The staff is knowledgeable and can answer questions about fees and requirements.

**Phone:** (817) 459-6599 for permit inquiries and fee information (Monday-Friday, 7 AM to 5 PM)

**Email:** Preliminary inquiries can be submitted to development@arlingtontx.gov, though official submissions must be made online or in-person.

### Permit Types and Costs

Arlington categorizes permits by project type and complexity:

- **Residential building permits:** $400-$1,500 for additions, renovations, and remodels
- **Commercial building permits:** $800-$5,000+ depending on square footage and use
- **Plan review fees:** $150-$400 depending on project complexity
- **Electrical permits:** $75-$175
- **Plumbing permits:** $100-$250
- **Mechanical/HVAC permits:** $100-$250
- **Roofing permits:** $75-$150
- **Sign permits:** $100-$300

The city maintains an [online fee calculator](https://arlingtontx.gov/development/fees) to estimate costs based on project valuation and type. Always verify current rates before quoting projects.

### Timeline Expectations

Arlington's permit review follows this predictable timeline:

1. **Submission** — Same day online, next business day in-person
2. **Completeness review** — 2-4 business days to verify all documents are submitted
3. **Plan review** — 8-15 business days for standard residential; 10-20 for commercial
4. **Corrections/resubmittals** — If required, add 5-10 business days per revision
5. **Permit issuance** — Same day upon final approval

Expedited review (50% surcharge) reduces timelines to approximately 5-10 business days for projects needing faster approval.
