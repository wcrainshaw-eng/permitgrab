## Spokane's Building Permit System

Spokane's Department of Building Services manages permits through a **comprehensive ArcGIS MapServer platform** that integrates building permits, planning permits, and engineering permits into one system.

### Where to Apply

**Online (Recommended):** Spokane's permit system is accessible at **spokanebuilds.org** through their ArcGIS MapServer interface. Online submission is available 24/7 and is the fastest method. New account setup takes 10 minutes.

**In-Person:** The Department of Building Services is located at **808 W. Spokane Falls Blvd, Spokane, WA 99201** (near the downtown core). Hours are Monday-Friday, 8 AM to 5 PM. Limited walk-in service is available; expect potential wait times.

**Phone Support:** (509) 625-6000 for permit assistance and technical questions (Monday-Friday, 8 AM to 5 PM)

**Email:** building_services@spokanecity.org for non-urgent questions

### Permit Costs in Spokane

Spokane's permit fees are reasonable and scale with project valuation:

- **Residential single-family addition:** $150-$1,000
- **Residential remodel:** $100-$1,200
- **Multifamily residential (per unit):** $300-$2,000
- **Commercial tenant improvement:** $600-$8,000
- **Commercial new construction:** $1,500-$20,000+
- **Engineering permits:** $200-$1,500
- **Plan review fees:** $100-$400
- **Inspection fees:** $50-$100 per inspection

Spokane publishes a detailed [fee schedule](https://www.spokanebuilds.org) that breaks down costs by permit type. The city's ArcGIS system displays estimated valuations for most permits, allowing contractors to quickly benchmark similar projects.

### Spokane's Permit Timeline

Spokane publishes average processing times for both standard and expedited review:

1. **Submission** — Immediate online, same-day in-person
2. **Initial review** — 7-14 business days for residential, 10-25 for commercial
3. **Plan corrections** — 5-7 business days per round if needed
4. **Permit issuance** — Upon final approval

Expedited review is available for an additional fee (approximately 20% surcharge) with 3-5 day turnaround for residential and 7-10 days for commercial.
