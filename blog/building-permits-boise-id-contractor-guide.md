## Boise's Building Permit System

Boise manages building permits through the **Community Planning & Development Department (CPD)**, which handles all residential, commercial, and specialty permits through a modern, accessible system.

### Where to Apply

**Online (Recommended):** The City of Boise's online permit portal is available at **boise.org/permits**. This is the fastest and most convenient way to submit permits, track project status, and upload required documents. The system is available 24/7 and significantly reduces processing time compared to in-person submission.

**In-Person:** Visit the Development Services Department at **150 N Capitol Blvd, Suite 600, Boise, ID 83702**. Hours are Monday-Friday, 8 AM to 4:30 PM. In-person submissions are accepted but may have longer wait times during peak periods.

**Phone Support:** (208) 384-4057 for permit inquiries and technical support (Monday-Friday, 8 AM to 5 PM)

### Permit Costs in Boise

Boise's permit fee structure is straightforward and based on project type and estimated construction value:

- **Residential remodel permits:** $400-$600
- **Residential addition permits:** $500-$900
- **Residential new construction:** $800-$1,500
- **Commercial permits:** $1,500-$8,000+ depending on valuation
- **Trade-specific permits:** $150-$400 (electrical, mechanical, plumbing)
- **Plan review fees:** $100-$800

The city's permit fee schedule is available on the CPD website and includes detailed breakdowns by project category.

### Boise's Permit Timeline

The city publishes average review times and has consistently improved processing speeds:

1. **Submission** — Same day online or next business day in-person
2. **Initial review** — 7-15 business days for residential, 10-25 for commercial
3. **Plan corrections** — If needed, 3-7 business days per revision round
4. **Permit issuance** — Upon final approval

Simple residential projects like electrical upgrades, mechanical replacements, or roof replacements may be issued in 3-5 days. More complex remodels and commercial projects can take 30+ days if revisions are required.
