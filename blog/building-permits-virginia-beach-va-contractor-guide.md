## Virginia Beach's Building Permit System

Virginia Beach processes permits through the **City of Virginia Beach Department of Planning & Community Development, Permitting Division**. This system is accessible, transparent, and comprehensive — tracking thousands of active projects with detailed information about construction types and work scope.

### Where to Apply

**Online (Recommended):** Virginia Beach offers online permit submission through their portal at **vbgov.com/permits**. This is the fastest and most convenient method for residential and commercial permits. Real-time tracking and automated status updates keep you informed throughout the review process.

**In-Person:** Visit the Permit Center at **One Columbus Center, 4th Floor, Virginia Beach, VA 23510** (near Military Circle). Hours are Monday-Friday, 8 AM to 5 PM. This location also has a one-stop shop for plan review questions. Expect 30-45 minute wait times during peak morning hours.

**Phone:** (757) 385-4410 for permit inquiries (Monday-Friday, 8 AM to 5 PM)

**Mail:** Permits can be submitted by mail, though this adds 7-10 business days to processing time.

### Permit Types and Costs

Virginia Beach categorizes permits by construction type and work scope:

- **Residential building permits:** $300-$1,500 for additions, renovations, and remodels
- **Commercial building permits:** $500-$5,000+ depending on square footage and construction scope
- **Plan review fees:** $100-$500 depending on project complexity
- **Electrical permits:** $50-$200
- **Plumbing permits:** $75-$250
- **Mechanical/HVAC permits:** $75-$250
- **Roofing permits:** $50-$150

The city maintains an [online fee schedule](https://vbgov.com/permits) with detailed breakdowns. Check current rates before submitting bids, as fees are updated annually.

### Timeline Expectations

Virginia Beach's review process follows this typical timeline:

1. **Submission** — Same day online, next business day in-person
2. **Initial completeness check** — 2-3 business days; staff verifies all required documents
3. **Plan review** — 7-12 business days for standard projects; complex projects take longer
4. **Resubmittals/corrections** — If required, add 5-8 business days per revision round
5. **Permit issuance** — Same day upon final approval

Expedited review options are available at additional cost (typically 50-100% surcharge) and can reduce review time to 5-7 business days for urgent projects.

Virginia Beach's construction pipeline is robust and diverse:

**Oceanfront & Waterfront Properties:** The beaches and waterfront areas command premium prices and drive steady renovation and new construction activity. Oceanfront contractors stay busy with exterior upgrades, deck work, and property improvements.

**I-64 Corridor Commercial:** The interstate corridor attracts office parks, medical facilities, and retail development. Commercial contractors have consistent work here from new construction and tenant improvement projects.

**Military-Related Construction:** Naval Station Norfolk and other military facilities drive consistent demand for commercial, industrial, and specialty construction. Government contracting opportunities exist for qualified contractors.

**Residential Neighborhoods:** Suburbs west of I-64 and the western growth corridor continue rapid residential development. Single-family and multifamily projects dominate this sector, creating opportunities for residential contractors and subcontractors.

**South Harbor District:** Emerging waterfront district with mixed-use development, residential, and commercial projects. This area is generating increasing permit activity as redevelopment accelerates.

**Retail & Commercial Tenant Improvements:** Shopping centers, office buildings, and commercial spaces require constant updates and renovations to meet tenant needs and code requirements.
