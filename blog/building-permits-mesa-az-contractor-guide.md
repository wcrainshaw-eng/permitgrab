## Mesa's Building Permit System

Mesa manages all building permits through the **Community Development Department**, which operates on the **Socrata platform** — one of the most transparent and contractor-friendly permit systems in Arizona.

### Where to Apply

**Online (Recommended):** Mesa's eCitizenAccess portal is available at **mesaaz.gov/permits**. This cloud-based Socrata system makes permit submission, tracking, and document management simple and efficient. The system is available 24/7 and includes detailed project information, contractor data, and valuation details that help contractors understand project scope and competition.

**In-Person:** Visit the Planning & Zoning Department at **155 N Center St, Suite 200, Mesa, AZ 85201**. Hours are Monday-Friday, 8 AM to 4 PM. In-person submission is available but takes longer than online processing.

**Phone Support:** (480) 644-3000 for permit inquiries and assistance (Monday-Friday, 8 AM to 5 PM)

### Permit Costs in Mesa

Mesa's permit fee structure is straightforward and based on estimated construction valuation:

- **Residential remodel permits:** $200-$600
- **Residential addition permits:** $350-$900
- **Residential new construction:** $500-$1,200+
- **Commercial permits:** $1,000-$10,000+ depending on valuation
- **Trade-specific permits:** $100-$400 (electrical, mechanical, plumbing)
- **Plan review fees:** $75-$600

The city's detailed fee schedule is available on the Community Development website and is updated annually.

### Mesa's Permit Timeline

Mesa has worked to streamline its review process and maintains industry-leading timelines:

1. **Submission** — Same day online or next business day in-person
2. **Initial review** — 5-12 business days for residential, 8-20 for commercial
3. **Plan corrections** — If required, 3-5 business days per revision
4. **Permit issuance** — Upon final approval

Simple residential projects like electrical upgrades, water heater replacements, or roof work may be issued in 2-4 days. Larger remodels, additions, and commercial projects may require 20-30+ days if design revisions are necessary.
