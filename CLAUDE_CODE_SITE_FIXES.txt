PART 6: LEAD SCORING FIX
========================================================================

Every permit currently shows "100 pts" — the scoring function is not differentiating. Implement real scoring:

SCORING FORMULA (0-100 points):

Recency (0-30 points):
  - Filed today: 30 pts
  - Filed within 3 days: 25 pts
  - Filed within 7 days: 20 pts
  - Filed within 14 days: 15 pts
  - Filed within 30 days: 10 pts
  - Filed within 60 days: 5 pts
  - Older than 60 days: 0 pts

Project Value (0-25 points):
  - Over $1M: 25 pts
  - $500K-$1M: 20 pts
  - $250K-$500K: 15 pts
  - $100K-$250K: 10 pts
  - $50K-$100K: 5 pts
  - Under $50K or unknown: 0 pts

Contact Completeness (0-20 points):
  - Has owner name: +8 pts
  - Has phone number: +6 pts
  - Has full address (not just city): +6 pts

Permit Stage (0-15 points):
  - Filed/Application (early = less competition): 15 pts
  - In Review: 12 pts
  - Permitted/Issued: 10 pts
  - In Progress: 5 pts
  - Final/Complete: 2 pts
  - Denied/Expired: 0 pts

Trade Specificity (0-10 points):
  - Specific trade classified (not General Construction): 10 pts
  - General Construction: 3 pts
  - Unknown/unclassified: 0 pts

DISPLAY:
- Keep the flame icon 🔥 but only show it for scores >= 70
- Color code: 80-100 = orange/hot, 60-79 = yellow, 40-59 = gray, below 40 = light gray
- Show the actual point value (not always 100)
- Re-score all existing permits when this deploys

========================================================================

Every permit card shows a green phone button with a phone number. These numbers appear auto-generated (random area codes that don't match the permit's city). Fake phone numbers destroy credibility.

Fix:
- If the phone number comes from actual permit data or a verified enrichment source → show it
- If the phone number is generated/synthetic → DO NOT show it
- For free users: show "📞 Contact Info" with a lock icon and "Upgrade for contact details" tooltip — this is BETTER for conversion than showing a fake number
