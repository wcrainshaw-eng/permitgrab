  Before: Footer "New York City" → /permits/new-york (showed state hub)

  resolve "new-york-city" to the NYC page. The city_configs slug change

  Fix: Either revert the slug to "new-york" and handle the state hub
  conflict differently (e.g., /states/new-york for the state hub), or
  ensure the route handler maps "new-york-city" to the correct DB data.
