TASK B: DEDUPLICATE BULK SOURCE REQUESTS (10 min)
===================================================================

This is the most important optimization. With 678 cities, many
share the same bulk source (e.g., 420 NJ cities all come from
nj_statewide). The collector must NOT hit the NJ endpoint 420
times — it should hit it ONCE and distribute permits to all cities.

Check how _collect_all_inner() handles this in V15/prod_cities mode:

  IF it iterates prod_cities one by one and calls fetch for each:
    → PROBLEM. It will hit nj_statewide 420 times.
    → FIX: Group prod_cities by source_id first.
           For each unique bulk source_id, call collect_bulk_source()
           ONCE, then distribute results to all cities sharing that source.
           For individual city sources, call fetch_permits() as normal.

  IF it already groups by source_id:
    → GOOD. Just verify it works correctly.

The pattern should be:
  1. Get all active prod_cities
  2. Group by source_id
  3. For each unique bulk source: collect once, split by city_field
  4. For each unique city source: collect individually
  5. Insert all permits
