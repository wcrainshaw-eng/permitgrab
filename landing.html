What Are Building Permit Leads?

Building permit leads are generated from public construction permit filings at city and county government offices. When a homeowner or contractor files for a building permit, that information becomes public record. PermitGrab aggregates these records from multiple municipalities and delivers them as actionable leads to contractors, home service companies, and suppliers who can provide services for those projects.

Every time someone files for a roofing permit, HVAC installation, electrical upgrade, plumbing work, or general construction project, a new lead is created. These are real people with real projects and real budgets, making permit leads some of the highest-intent sales opportunities available to contractors.

Why Permit Leads Beat Other Lead Sources

Unlike paid advertising leads or door-knocking, building permit leads come from verified projects with committed budgets. The homeowner has already decided to build or renovate — they've gone through the effort of pulling permits. This means higher conversion rates, shorter sales cycles, and less wasted time on tire-kickers.

Traditional lead generation for contractors (HomeAdvisor, Angi, Thumbtack) charges per lead and often sells the same lead to 5-10 competitors. PermitGrab gives you access to the raw data — the same permits — so you can reach property owners directly, on your own terms, without competing on a bidding platform.
