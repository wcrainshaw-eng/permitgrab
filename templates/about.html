About PermitGrab

Helping contractors find their next job through public permit data

Our Mission

PermitGrab was built with one goal: help contractors and construction professionals find leads faster. Every day, thousands of building permits are filed with city governments — each one representing a potential job opportunity. But finding this data meant navigating dozens of different municipal websites, each with their own search interfaces and data formats.

We built PermitGrab to solve this problem. We aggregate permit data from {{ city_count }}+ cities across the United States, normalize it into a consistent format, and deliver it to contractors who can actually use it to grow their businesses.

How It Works

Data Collection

We pull permit data daily from official municipal open data portals, including Socrata, ArcGIS, and CKAN platforms.

Processing & Enrichment

Raw permit data is cleaned, normalized, and enriched with trade classifications, lead scores, and contact information where available.

Delivery

Access permits through our web dashboard, receive daily email alerts, or export to CSV for your CRM.

Why Contractors Trust Us

Unlike scraped or synthetic data, every permit in PermitGrab comes directly from official government sources. We're transparent about where our data comes from, and we update it daily to ensure you're seeing the freshest leads possible.

Our lead scoring algorithm helps you prioritize the best opportunities — high-value projects, recently filed permits, and those with complete contact information rise to the top.
