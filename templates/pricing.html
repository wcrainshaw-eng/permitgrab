What's included in the free plan?
The free plan lets you browse all building permits, filter by city, trade, and value, and see permit details and project values. Contact information is available with the Professional plan.

Yes, you can cancel your subscription anytime from your account settings. No contracts, no hidden fees. Your access continues until the end of your billing period.

Our permit data is updated daily from official municipal sources. We pull directly from city and county building department APIs to ensure you're seeing the latest permits.
